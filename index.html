WaveDict

Get japanese dictionary definitions right in a wave.

How to use.

To use WaveDict, simply add

wavedict@appspot.com

to your contacts and invite it to a wave.

To get a definition, simply type

define: word

into a blip. Click done, and WaveDict will get the definition for you!

Sources.

WaveDict currently get's it's definitions from Yahoo! 辞書.
